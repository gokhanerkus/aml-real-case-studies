# Transaction Flow Analysis

## Observed Patterns

### 1. Concentrated Financial Exposure
- Majority of credit allocated to a single group  

➡️ Indicator:
Related-party risk  

---

### 2. Cross-Border Transfers
- Funds move between multiple countries  
- Includes high-risk jurisdictions  

➡️ Indicator:
Sanctions evasion risk  

---

### 3. Use of Financial Instruments
- Bonds and structured financing used  
- Potential indirect funding channels  

➡️ Indicator:
Layering mechanism  

---

### 4. Intermediary Routing
- Transactions routed through multiple entities  

➡️ Indicator:
Obfuscation of source and destination
