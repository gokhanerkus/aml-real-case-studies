# Case 02 — Banking Network Case

## Overview

This case examines a financial structure involving a Turkish bank and multiple related entities engaged in cross-border transactions.

The structure shows potential links to an Iran-connected financial network.

---

## Scenario

- A financial institution is observed to have significant exposure to a specific corporate group  
- The same network is linked to cross-border transactions involving sanctioned jurisdictions  
- Large-scale fund transfers are routed through intermediary structures  

---

## Key Elements

- Concentration of credit exposure to a single group  
- Use of financial instruments (e.g. bonds, transfers)  
- Cross-border movement involving multiple jurisdictions  
- Links to individuals previously associated with sanctions evasion cases  

---

## Objective

To assess whether the observed structure presents indicators of:
- Money laundering  
- Sanctions evasion  
- Abuse of financial institutions
