# Red Flags

## High-Risk Indicators

- High concentration of loans to a single group  
- Cross-border transfers involving high-risk jurisdictions  
- Links to individuals associated with sanctions cases  
- Use of complex financial structures  
- Potential regulatory gaps or weak oversight  

---

## AML Typologies

- Network-based money laundering  
- Sanctions evasion  
- Layering through financial instruments
