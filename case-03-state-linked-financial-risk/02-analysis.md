# Risk Analysis

## 1. Political Exposure (PEP Risk)

The involvement of senior figures within public financial institutions indicates potential political exposure.

➡️ Risk:
Influence over financial decision-making processes  

---

## 2. Concentration Risk

Financial resources appear to be directed toward a limited number of corporate groups.

➡️ Risk:
Reduced diversification and potential preferential allocation  

---

## 3. Governance Risk

Reported patterns suggest possible limitations in independent oversight mechanisms.

➡️ Risk:
Weak internal controls and decision transparency  

---

## 4. Systemic Risk

State-linked financial structures may amplify risk across the broader financial system.

➡️ Risk:
Contagion and institutional vulnerability  

---

## Risk Level: HIGH

### Key Drivers:
- Political exposure  
- Resource concentration  
- Governance concerns
