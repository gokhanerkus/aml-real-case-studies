# Case 03 — State-Linked Financial Risk & Political Exposure

## Summary
This case examines reported relationships between public financial institutions, senior banking figures, and specific corporate groups.

It focuses on governance, concentration of financial resources, and potential political exposure risks within the financial system.

---

## Key Focus Areas
- Political exposure (PEP risk)  
- Concentration risk  
- Governance and oversight  
- State-linked financial structures  

---

## Why This Case Matters
This case highlights how financial systems may be exposed to risk through:
- Concentrated allocation of financial resources  
- Close relationships between financial and political actors  
- Reduced transparency in decision-making processes
